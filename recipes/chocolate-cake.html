Chocolate Cake

Description

Our family's beloved chocolate cake is a masterpiece of moistness and richness, crafted into three sumptuous layers. Each tier is generously filled with whipped cream and encased in a decadent chocolate buttercream frosting. This cake has been a perennial favorite in our home, its indulgent flavors and textures delighting guests and family alike over the years.

To elevate this dessert, consider garnishing with a variety of toppings such as colorful sprinkles, delicate chocolate curls, or seasonal fresh fruits. These additions not only add visual appeal but also enhance the cake's flavors, creating a truly memorable experience for any occasion.

Ingredients

    Cake:

    • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 2 cups boiling water
    • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 teaspoons baking soda
    • ½ teaspoon salt
    • ½ teaspoon baking powder
    • 2 ½ cups white sugar
    • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
    • 4 large eggs
    • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract

    Frosting:

    • ⅓ cup unsalted butter, softened
    • 2 cups confectioners' sugar
    • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    Filling:

    • 1 pint heavy whipping cream
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
